Live music licensing on-demand

For over a century now, Performing Rights Organizations (i.e. ASCAP, BMI, SESAC) have required that music venues
pay for multiple long-term performance licenses, using threats and litigation as the means to enforce these rules.
This has introduced a barrier to entry that has pushed many existing and would-be venues out of the market,
artificially restricting the supply and thus limiting opportunities for musicians and DJs as well.
With a radical new model, SongFoo empowers performers to take their careers into their own hands
by allowing them to self-license and thus perform anywhere, at any time.

It's time to free the music, and we need your feedback!
